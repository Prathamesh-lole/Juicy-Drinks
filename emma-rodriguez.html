About Emma

Emma Rodriguez is Juicy's Sustainability Director, leading the company's commitment to environmental responsibility and ethical business practices. With a Master's degree in Environmental Science and over 10 years of experience in sustainable business operations, Emma ensures that every aspect of Juicy's operations aligns with the company's values of protecting our planet.

Emma joined Juicy in 2020, bringing extensive experience from her previous role as Environmental Consultant for Fortune 500 companies. Her passion for environmental advocacy began during her college years when she witnessed the devastating effects of industrial pollution on local communities. This experience shaped her career focus on creating business solutions that benefit both people and the planet.

Under Emma's leadership, Juicy has achieved carbon neutrality, implemented zero-waste manufacturing processes, and established partnerships with organic farms that practice regenerative agriculture. Her holistic approach to sustainability encompasses everything from ingredient sourcing to packaging design, ensuring that Juicy's environmental impact is not just minimized, but actively positive.
