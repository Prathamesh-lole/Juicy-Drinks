About Mike

Mike Chen is Juicy's Head of Innovation, bringing a unique blend of food science expertise and creative culinary vision to every product development project. With a Ph.D. in Food Science from UC Davis and over 12 years of experience in the beverage industry, Mike is the mastermind behind Juicy's most popular and groundbreaking flavor combinations.

Before joining Juicy, Mike worked as a senior food scientist at leading beverage companies, where he specialized in functional ingredient integration and flavor optimization. His passion for creating beverages that deliver both exceptional taste and nutritional benefits made him the perfect fit for Juicy's mission-driven approach.

Mike's innovative approach combines traditional food science principles with cutting-edge research in nutrition and flavor chemistry. He's responsible for developing Juicy's proprietary blending techniques that preserve maximum nutritional value while creating complex, satisfying flavor profiles that keep customers coming back.
