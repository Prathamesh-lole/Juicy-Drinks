About Sarah

Sarah Johnson is the visionary founder and CEO of Juicy, bringing over 15 years of experience in nutrition science and sustainable business practices. Her journey began as a certified nutritionist working with athletes and health-conscious individuals, where she discovered the transformative power of properly crafted, nutrient-dense beverages.

After witnessing firsthand how the right combination of fruits and vegetables could dramatically improve her clients' energy levels and overall well-being, Sarah became passionate about making these benefits accessible to everyone. She founded Juicy in 2018 with a simple yet powerful mission: to prove that healthy choices can be the most delicious ones.

Under Sarah's leadership, Juicy has grown from a small local operation to a recognized brand known for its commitment to organic ingredients, innovative flavors, and sustainable practices. Her background in nutrition ensures that every Juicy product not only tastes exceptional but also delivers maximum nutritional value.
